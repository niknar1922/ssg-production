Why SSG

A better schedule creates a better program.

We built SSG for the people running the programs — not the finance office.

Calendar-first by design
Your program calendar is the starting point for everything — not an afterthought. Every tool flows from your schedule, not the other way around.
One platform, not six
Stop juggling SignUpGenius, GoFundMe, Eventbrite, Shopify, and a spreadsheet. SSG brings communications, ticketing, fundraising, and apparel under one roof.
Built to reduce burnout
Operational clarity is not a luxury — it's what keeps good directors in their programs long-term. SSG is designed to return margin to the people who carry the most.
Daily utility, not seasonal
Budgets are reviewed monthly. Fundraisers happen twice a year. Calendars drive daily behavior — which is why SSG earns a place in your routine instead of collecting dust.
For directors, boosters, and families
SSG creates shared visibility across everyone connected to the program — so no one is chasing information and parents stay informed without extra work from the director.
Budget as output, not input
When your schedule is clear, the revenue and costs follow naturally. SSG shows you the financial picture as a result of your program plan — not as a constraint you have to work around.
