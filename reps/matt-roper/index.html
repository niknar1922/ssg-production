Our Approach
The Budget is the Strategy
“Your program doesn’t have a fundraising problem. It has a budgeting problem. Once you know exactly what you need — we build the campaign to match.” Edit with Matt
Most directors set fundraising goals based on last year’s number or gut feel. Neither is a budget.

Driven Fundraising starts every relationship with a free budget session. We map your district account, your booster account, your real expenses. The gap between where you are and where you want to be — that’s your campaign goal. Not a guess. A number with a job.
